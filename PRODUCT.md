# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary audience is **merchants** — business owners and managers completing card-processing onboarding (locations, banking, identity verification, and e-signature).

Secondary audience is the **Cliqbux team** (sales agents and admins). They stage applications from Admin → Applications and may impersonate a merchant to help fill forms. Design decisions optimize for the merchant experience; agent tooling supports that flow rather than competing with it.

## Product Purpose

Cliqbux E-Onboarding is the portal where merchants complete the work required to get approved for card processing: organization and locations, bank connection, identity verification, and signing the merchant agreement — then submit.

Success means two things at once: the merchant can finish in one sitting without calling support, and the application reaches ready-to-sign / submitted with clean data (not stuck incomplete in MSPWare or the portal).

## Positioning

The trusted place merchants complete KYC, banking, and e-sign for Cliqbux.

## Brand Personality

Fast, modern, confident. Serious financial work should feel decisive and contemporary — never cold bureaucracy, and never playful consumer novelty.

## Anti-references

- Generic purple / gradient “AI SaaS” dashboards
- Busy traditional bank portals with cluttered forms and dense chrome
- Playful consumer apps heavy on illustration, bounce, and novelty

## Design Principles

1. **Trust under pressure** — Merchants hand over SSN, bank details, and signatures. Clarity and calm control beat visual spectacle.
2. **Finish without a call** — Every step should be understandable enough that a motivated merchant completes alone; agent help is backup, not the default path.
3. **Fast confidence** — Momentum and modern craft; no theatrical motion or decorative complexity that slows the job.
4. **Merchant owns the screen** — Agent staging and impersonation exist to assist; the primary UX still reads as the merchant’s application, not an internal ops console.
5. **Clean data is part of done** — A beautiful incomplete application is a failure. Design reinforces completeness and readiness to sign/submit.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Practical bar: solid contrast, keyboard-usable flows, and respect for `prefers-reduced-motion`. No formal WCAG claim yet.
