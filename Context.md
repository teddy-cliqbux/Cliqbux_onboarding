#Current project: Merchant onboarding and management center
## What we are building: Our old systems for onboarding a new client are disparate and siloed. We are creating a modern and seamless onboarding and management experience that ties together many tools and vendors on our backend to present a clean and easy experience to our new and existing users.
## What good looks like: A successful output is simple to use for our clients, is safe and secure, and is extremely reliable. It doesn't require more logins, nor expert understanding of the payment processing industry to use.
## What to avoid: Cluttered UI, double data entry, multiple logins, lack of visibility from Cliqbux team.
